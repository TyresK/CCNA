<h1>CCNA Lab Day 1</h1>

<h2>Overview</h2>
The goal of this first lab was to become familiar with the Cisco Packet Tracer environment and understand the basic network devices that will be used throughout the CCNA course.<br><br>

During this lab I explored the Packet Tracer interface, reviewed available networking devices, and recreated a simple multi-site network topology to understand how enterprise networks are structured.<br><br>

No configurations were performed in this lab — the focus was on device identification, topology layout, and Packet Tracer navigation.
